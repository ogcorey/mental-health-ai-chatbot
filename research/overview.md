# Research Overview: AI Mental Health Chatbot

## Problem Statement

Many individuals experiencing mental health challenges lack immediate access to support. AI chatbots can provide accessible, real-time interaction, but must be carefully designed to handle sensitive situations responsibly.

## Research Objectives

* Understand how AI can simulate supportive conversations
* Analyze sentiment detection techniques for emotional classification
* Explore chatbot personalization for user engagement
* Evaluate risks in handling sensitive mental health data

## Key Areas of Focus

* Sentiment analysis (positive, negative, crisis states)
* Prompt engineering for empathetic responses
* User behavior and engagement patterns
* Ethical AI and data privacy considerations

## Initial Findings

* Personalized interactions improve engagement
* Prompt tone significantly affects user trust
* Rule-based crisis detection is necessary alongside AI models

## Next Steps

* Define chatbot response strategies
* Begin prompt testing and refinement
* Design backend architecture
